THE PROCESS


As the sole creator of Bubble, all responsibilities will fall upon me. I started by brainstorming project ideas. After many drafts were left behind, one project stood out- Bubble. I began by prototyping the web designs in Figma, and getting an understanding of the database designs through diagramining.

After the initial drafts were created, I brought my project fourth for review by peers and professionals. Several implementations were changed to better the design, into what Bubble will be.
